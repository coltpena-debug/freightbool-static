Common Trailer Sizes

53' Dry Van: 26-30 std pallets. 48' Reefer: 24 pallets. 28' Pup: 12-14 pallets. Flatbed: 48' or 53'. Step-deck: 48' for tall loads.

Weight Limits (Federal)

Gross: 80,000 lbs total. Steer axle: 12,000 lbs. Drive tandem: 34,000 lbs. Trailer tandem: 34,000 lbs. State permits may allow higher.

NMFC Class Range

Classes go 50, 55, 60, 65, 70, 77.5, 85, 92.5, 100, 110, 125, 150, 175, 200, 250, 300, 400, 500. Lower class = denser = cheaper to ship.

HOS Daily Limits

11-hour drive limit after 10 hrs off-duty. 14-hour on-duty window. 30-min break after 8 cumulative hours. 60/70 hr in 7/8 day cycle.

Standard Detention

2 hours free at shipper/consignee. After: $50-$100/hour LTL, $75-$100/hour FTL is industry standard. Always confirm rate up-front.

FSC Calculation

Standard formula: ((Current diesel - $1.20 base) ÷ MPG) × miles. Most carriers update weekly using DOE national avg.

SCAC Code

Standard Carrier Alpha Code. 2-4 letter unique ID assigned by NMFTA. Required on most BOLs and EDI transactions.

Required BOL Fields

Shipper, consignee, carrier, commodity description, weight, # pieces, NMFC + class (LTL), declared value if applicable, freight terms, signatures.

TONU Standard

Truck Order Not Used. When carrier dispatched but load cancels. Industry standard: $150-$250 minimum, varies by lane.
